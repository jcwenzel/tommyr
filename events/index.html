Venues we have used in the past.

Katherine Legge Memorial Lodge

  • 5901 S Countyline Road, Hinsdale IL 60521
  • villageofhinsdale.org/klm
  • klm@villageofhinsdale.org
  • (630) 789-7095

The Katherine Legge Memorial Park and Lodge (Legge pronounced "leg") are located on 52 beautiful acres of woodland, a perfect setting for any occasion. The first floor of this English-style building provides a spacious living room with fireplace, dining room, kitchen, restrooms and office. The second floor is a large ballroom with fireplace which was used for recreational activities. Today, the Lodge and grounds are available for rental to accommodate private and corporate events such as weddings and receptions, meetings, parties, banquets, picnics, etc. On February 13, 2008, the Illinois Council of American Institute of Architects (AIA) recognized Katherine Legge Memorial Park and Lodge as one of 150 "Great Places in Illinois".

Graue Mill (Graue House)

  • 3800 York Road Oak Brook, Illinois 60523
  • grauemill.org
  • info@grauemill.org
  • (630) 920-9720

The Frederick Graue House, standing adjacent to Graue Mill and Museum, is located within Fullersburg Woods Forest Preserve in Oak Brook. The exterior of the house represents a historically accurate restoration. The interior has been tastefully renovated to be an adaptive reuse of the space. The first floor has three large rooms and a service area that are available for public use on a rental basis. The second floor houses the offices of the Museum.

Danada House

  • 3S501 Naperville Rd Wheaton, IL 60189
  • danadahouse.com
  • info@danadahouse.com
  • (630) 668-5392

Commodity trader Daniel F. Rice and his wife Ada built this sprawling, white brick, 19-room country home in 1939. This was the home farm of the 1965 Kentucky Derby winner Lucky Debonair, ridden by jockey Bill Shoemaker. Danada House accommodates up to 250 guests in the Atrium or up to 90 guests in the main level of the House. The flexible floor plan makes Danada House a unique reception hall and meeting facility for your event.

Hinsdale Community House

  • 415 W. Eighth Street, Hinsdale, IL, 60521
  • thecommunityhouse.org
  • info@thecommunityhouse.org
  • (630) 323-7500

Hinsdale Community House is the only local nonprofit agency that provides quality recreation and vital social services. Based in Hinsdale and serving the surrounding communities, they provide recreation and cultural activities, counseling and therapeutic services, education support, and community-building opportunities. For over 70 years, without tax support, they have been there to serve you.
