The 411 on Tommy R’s Catering

Tommy R’s Catering has been putting together successful events across the the Chicagoland area for over 20 years. The list of event types we cater is never-ending - weddings, corporate brunches (and lunches), holiday parties, graduation parties, public events, private events, big events, small events... If you can dream it, we can make it a reality.

We take a unique approach to catering... we customize a menu for your event because we believe not all events are the same. At Tommy R’s Catering we work with you to tailor a menu perfect for your event.
