Tommy R is who you want crafting your next special event...

Thomas Romano acquired his culinary style and event planning ideology through practical experience. At the impressionable age of 12, Tommy worked as a busboy at his family’s Italian restaurant in Des Plaines, Illinois. Fueled by his love of great food and with the encouragement of his family, he attended Washburn trade school in Chicago, where he received his Certified Chef Certificate, and studied Business Administration at Harper College in Palatine.

Romano polished his skills as a kitchen supervisor for Errant Enterprises, which owned and operated a number of family restaurants. He worked his way up to the position of Multi-Unit Chef while running two restaurants; the Chicago Claim Company and the Zarrosta Grill, both in Oak Brook, Illinois.

In 1990, he branched out and opened Tommy R’s Italia in downtown Hinsdale. The restaurant grew from one storefront to three and built a loyal following of happy, well-fed customers. After ten wonderful and successful years, Tommy R’s Italia closed its doors, allowing Romano to expand his horizons within the hospitality industry. He freelanced with several prominent Chicago caterers including Wolfgang Puck, and Greg Christian. In 2004, Romano opened Events by Tommy R’s, which focuses on full service, off-premise catering and event planning. Through creative success and wonderful food, Romano has expanded his business to Tommy R’s To Go, which offers a fantastic and flavorful menu for pick-up or drop-off.

Romano opened Piccolo by Tommy R’s in 2009, a quaint kiosk located at 2001 Butterfield Road in Downers Grove. Open for breakfast and lunch, Piccolo offers a variety of gourmet coffee, sandwiches, and salads for the on-the-go crowd.

Romano is a proud member of Catersource, the leading continuing education resource for caterers worldwide. He also shares his extensive culinary knowledge while teaching at the College of Dupage, performing private demonstrations for a range of clients, and as a judge at numerous High School competitions.

We are proud to present our new name as Tommy R’s Catering along with our new location. We are now serving our clients in a more central and convenient location at 825 W. 75th Street in Willowbrook!
